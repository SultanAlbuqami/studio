# **App Name**: Stratagem Insights

## Core Features:

- Executive Dashboard UI: Render the comprehensive 'Control Tower' dashboard with clear hierarchy and premium visual styling for all required sections, including a hero section and dedicated areas for KPIs, portfolio health, intervention, and account exposure.
- Dynamic KPI Metrics: Display 4-6 critical Key Performance Indicators such as 'Orders in flight', 'On-time delivery %', 'Accepted value MTD', and 'Revenue at risk' in concise, elegant cards for quick executive overview.
- Portfolio Health Visualization: Present 'Weekly execution trend' and 'Portfolio status distribution' using sophisticated, executive-friendly charts that prioritize decision-making over raw data.
- Actionable Intervention Queue: Highlight 'Immediate intervention queue' and 'Top at-risk accounts' for focused attention and rapid identification of priorities for intervention.
- Customer Exposure Analysis: Provide a clean, detailed table showcasing 'Revenue at risk by family' and other account-specific exposure data.
- Minimalist Contextual Filters: Implement a non-intrusive and streamlined filter experience to allow executives to easily refine data views by high-level contexts without visual clutter or heavy sidebar weight.
- AI-Powered Executive Brief: A generative AI tool that synthesizes complex dashboard data into a concise, prioritized executive brief outlining current delivery status, identified risks, and immediate intervention needs, enabling understanding in under 2 minutes.

## Style Guidelines:

- Background color: A very dark desaturated blue-grey (#1B1E26), establishing a deep, sophisticated 'control tower' aesthetic suitable for an executive environment.
- Primary color: A clear, professional blue (#468CC8), used for key indicators, interactive elements, and positive data accents, signifying trust and reliability within the enterprise context.
- Accent color: A soft, refined lavender-blue (#B8B8EB), applied sparingly to draw attention to critical details or high-priority items without creating visual clutter, maintaining a premium feel.
- Headline and body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, neutral, and highly legible characteristics, ensuring executive-grade clarity across all text and numerical data points on the dashboard.
- Icons: Minimalistic line-art style icons, complementing numerical data in KPI cards and providing clear visual cues for different sections, aligned with a professional and low-visual-noise aesthetic.
- Layout: Grid-based design emphasizing strong whitespace, disciplined section separation, and generous padding to ensure an uncluttered, high-end, and easily scannable information hierarchy with fewer but stronger sections.
- Animation: Subtle and functional transitions for state changes, data updates, and hover effects, maintaining a professional demeanor and enhancing usability and flow without being distracting or playful.
